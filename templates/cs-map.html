Theoretical Computer Science

Algorithms

Algorithms!

An algorithm is a set of step-by-step instructions. Popular examples include sorting algorithms, search algorithms, graph traversal algorithms, and data compressio algorithms. The study of algorithms teaches you how to design and implement algorithms, and how to prove properties such as correctness or efficiency.
